Nagarkot

Nagarkot is located in the Kathmandu Valley of Nepal, at the east of Kathmandu.

Dhulikhel

Most of the Kathmandu valley is occupied by Dhulikhel Municipality. Dhulikhel municipality is one of the starting points for the hiking.

Tansen (Palpa)

ansen give an authentic welcome to Nepal with its ancient culture and fine Himalayan view from the Srinagar Hill.

Pokhara

Pokhara, also known as the city of eight lakes is the tourism capital of Nepal.

Chandragiri

The Chandragiri hill lies 7 Kms from Thankot at the west of Kathmandu valley.
